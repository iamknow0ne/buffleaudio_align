one job, done clearly

Line up the stack while keeping the performance alive.

Align watches the Guide and Dub, estimates a signed timing offset, scores phrase confidence, and lets you audition exactly what changed before trusting the move.

studio flow

Four controls that match the way vocal stacks are made.

01

Pick the role

Double Tight, Choir Natural, Rap Stack, and ADR Loose set the starting feel before manual tuning. Some stereo controls are still preview shaping while deeper DSP lands.

02

Trust the read

Phrase health and confidence keep the nudge button honest when the material is too short or noisy.

03

A/B the change

Original, Aligned, and Difference modes make the timing move audible, while the Changed meter shows the broad preview delta.

04

Clean the edges

Consonant Tamer Lite softens unmatched Dub attacks while preserving the vowel body.
