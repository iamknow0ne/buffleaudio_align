The App

One cockpit for the timing decision that matters.

Align keeps the producer focused on the Guide/Dub relationship: signal state, estimated offset, confidence, and the nudge to audition before committing to heavier alignment work.

Workflow

Built for doubles, stacks, and backing vocals.

01

Route

Place Align on the Dub and feed the Guide through sidechain, or use fallback monitoring while setting up.

02

Measure

Envelope and onset cues drive the first global offset estimate with visible confidence feedback.

03

Nudge

Preview a manual or suggested delay-safe move in milliseconds before printing or deeper editing.

04

Refine

The roadmap moves next toward capture buffers, A/B preview, and Consonant Tamer Lite.
